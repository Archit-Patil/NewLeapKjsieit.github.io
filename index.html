# We Believe

New Leap Initiative was formed in 2017 with a motive to enable students to build satellites through inexpensive, easily accessible components. The project facilitates as a STEM education tool to increase the involvement of students in space-tech and rf-communication domains. Since the formation of New Leap Initiative in 2017, students from different engineering branches at the institute have been working upon the development of a nano-satellite and ground-station for it. The students from the team are also involved with Make In India projects. The students from the team are also involved with Make In India projects.

ABOUT

New Leap Initiative was formed in 2017 with a motive to enable students to build satellites through inexpensive, easily accessible components. The project facilitates as a STEM education tool to increase the involvement of students in space-tech and rf-communication domains. Since the formation of New Leap Initiative in 2017, students from different engineering branches at the institute have been working upon the development of a nano-satellite and ground-station for it. The students from the team are also involved with Make In India projects.

OUR PARTNERS

Belief Sat 1

BeliefSat-1 is a NanoSat based on the PicoBus standards. The satellite is a 2P satellite which measures at 10 cm*5cm*5cm. The mission demonstates the use of radios based on LORA technology. It also demonstrates the use of commercially available electronic componets which are inexpensive and easily accessible.
